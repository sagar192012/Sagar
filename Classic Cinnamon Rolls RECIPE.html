### 🍞 **Classic Cinnamon Rolls**

#### **Ingredients**

**For the Dough:**

  • 1 cup warm milk (about 110°F / 43°C)
  • 2¼ tsp active dry yeast (1 packet)
  • ½ cup granulated sugar
  • 2 large eggs
  • ⅓ cup unsalted butter, melted
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 4 to 4½ cups all-purpose flour

**For the Filling:**

  • ⅔ cup brown sugar, packed
  • 2½ tbsp ground cinnamon
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened

**For the Cream Cheese Frosting:**

  • 4 oz cream cheese, softened
  • ¼ cup unsalted butter, softened
  • 1 cup powdered sugar
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • Pinch of salt
### 🧑‍🍳 **Instructions**
#### 1. **Make the Dough**
  1. In a large bowl, combine warm milk and yeast. Let sit 5–10 minutes until foamy.
  2. Add sugar, eggs, melted butter, and salt. Mix to combine.
  3. li>Add 4 cups of flour and mix until dough forms. Add up to ½ cup more flour as needed.
  4. Knead for 5–7 minutes (by hand or stand mixer) until smooth and elastic.
  5. Place in a greased bowl, cover, and let rise for 1–1.5 hours until doubled.
#### 2. **Prepare Filling**

* Mix brown sugar and cinnamon in a small bowl.

#### 3. **Assemble Rolls**
  1. Roll dough into a rectangle (\~16x12 inches).
  2. Spread softened butter on top, then sprinkle with cinnamon sugar.
  3. Roll tightly from the long edge. Slice into 12 even pieces.
  4. Place in a greased 9x13-inch baking dish. Cover and let rise for 30–45 minutes.
#### 4. **Bake**

* Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Bake for 20–25 minutes until golden brown

.

#### 5. **Make Frosting**

* Beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Add powdered sugar, vanilla, and salt. Mix until fluffy.

#### 6. **Frost and Serve**

* Spread frosting on warm rolls and enjoy!
