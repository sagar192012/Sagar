The first words of a welcome message form a formal greeting to the website visitor. Inform the audience in explicit terms that you are thankful for their virtual visit. Your choice of a greeting can depend on your brand identity. For example, on a website for a hotel, the language may be more formal, such as "Welcome to Orphe Hotel and Suites." On a website for a women's boutique, the language may be informal, such as "Hey, girl! Welcome to Crystal's Clothes!" The greeting can also include a catchphrase or slogan that represents the brand.
